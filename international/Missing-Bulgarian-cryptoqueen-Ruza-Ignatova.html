Missing Bulgarian cryptoqueen Ruza Ignatova

लापता बुल्गारियाई क्रिप्टोक़्वीन रुजा इग्नातोवा

Ruza Ignatova had defrauded investors of 4.5 billion dollars in the name of investing money in cryptocurrency and after that, where did she disappear, did Ruza Ignatova enjoy spending the billions of dollars stolen from others on herself, or He was killed by the same Bulgarian underworld people.

रुजा इग्नातोवा ने क्रिप्टोकरेंसी में पैसे लगाने के नाम पर निवेशकों को 4.5 अरब डॉलर का चूना लगाया था और इसके बाद वो जाने कहां गुम हो गईं,क्या रुजा इग्नातोवा दूसरों से चोरी किए गए,अरबों डॉलर ख़ुद पर ख़र्च करके लुत्फ़ ले पाईं, या फिर उनको उन्हीं बुल्गारियाई अंडरवर्ल्ड के लोगों ने मार डाला.

Ruza, who studied at Oxford University, was born in Bulgaria and grew up in Germany. Before launching a cryptocurrency named OneCoin in 2014, Ruja had made a brilliant career in the finance sector. Ruza Ignatova had convinced millions of people around the world to invest in OneCoin. He had clearly shown everyone that in return for this investment, he would get many times more profit than the early investors of Bitcoin. But in reality, Ruza Ignatova, popularly known as Dr. Ruza, cleverly carried out an investment fraud of which there is no digital record as is the case with legitimate cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin. As German and US investigators tightened their grip, one morning in October 2017, Ruza Ignatova boarded a Ryanair flight from Bulgaria's capital, Sofia, to Athens. She was not seen since then. Over the past several years, BBC Eye Investigations and Panorama, along with a number of investigative teams around the world, have been trying to gather more information about what happened to Ruza and whether she is still alive. The most important link for this were those people who were closest to him. First, Richard Rinehart of America's Internal Revenue Service, along with the FBI, started an investigation against OneCoin. Richard told about an important person related to this case, whose name had never been taken publicly by the investigators before. This was the person who was given the responsibility of keeping Ruza Ignatova safe. Talkie was mentioned several times during the investigation. It was not that his name came only once. He was mentioned again and again during the investigation. What Richard said was similar to the information we already had. In 2019, US government lawyers said that the head of Ruza Ignatova's security team was the head of a major gang in the organized crime underworld in Bulgaria. However, American lawyers did not name him. An assistant attorney of the US government had said, we have evidence that a big name in drug smuggling in Bulgaria is closely linked to OneCoin. He was the personal security guard of Ruza Ignatova. A day earlier in the court, another lawyer of the US government had said that this was the same head of Ruza's security team who was also involved in Ruza's disappearance. According to Richard Rinehart, Ruja was such a vicious criminal that people have no idea about her. He says, this is just like a money laundering mafia or drug smuggler running on the power of steroids. It seems that this is also supported by the leaked documents of Europol. These documents have also been seen by BBC World Service's Eye Investigations
